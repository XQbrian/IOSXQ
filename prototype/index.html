1. Introduction

Welcome to Acme Corporation. This handbook describes our policies, procedures, and workplace standards effective January 1, 2026. It applies to all full-time, part-time, and contract employees.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional value to our clients while fostering a workplace grounded in trust, innovation, and shared accountability. Employees are expected to read this handbook thoroughly and acknowledge receipt within their first week.

2. Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to uphold the following standards at all times:

  • Integrity — Act honestly in all business dealings; disclose conflicts of interest promptly.
  • Respect — Treat all colleagues, clients, and partners with dignity regardless of role, background, or opinion.
  • Accountability — Own your commitments. Communicate early when timelines or scope changes.
  • Confidentiality — Safeguard company information. Do not share internal documents outside authorized channels.
  • Safety — Report unsafe conditions, incidents, or near-misses immediately to your manager or HR.

3. Remote Work Policy

3.1 Eligibility

Remote work is available to all permanent employees who have completed a 90-day onboarding period and whose role does not require on-site presence.

3.2 Requirements

  • Maintain a secure, private workspace with a reliable internet connection (min. 25 Mbps)
  • Use company-approved VPN and endpoint security at all times
  • Be available during core hours (10 AM – 3 PM local time) for collaboration
  • Attend all required team meetings via video with camera on

3.3 Equipment

Company-issued devices must be used for all work tasks. Personal device use requires explicit MDM enrollment approval from IT Security.

4. Data Security & AI Policy

All corporate data is classified under our four-tier sensitivity framework: Public, Internal, Confidential, and Restricted. Employees must handle data according to its classification level at all times.

🔐 XQ SECURE WORKSPACES POLICY

All files classified Confidential or Restricted are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM with keys stored in the Apple Secure Enclave. Keys never leave hardware.

PHI and CUI processed on-device only. Cloud AI requires dual authorization: enterprise policy opt-in AND content classification permit. Cited control: HIPAA §164.502, NIST AC-3

Approved AI tools (as of Q1 2026):

  • Microsoft Copilot for M365 — Internal and below only
  • XQ Secure Workspaces AI — All classification levels (local inference)
  • GitHub Copilot — Non-production code only; no credentials or secrets

5. Benefits & Compensation

Acme Corporation offers a comprehensive benefits package to all full-time employees beginning on their first day of employment.
